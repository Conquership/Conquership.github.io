About

Conquership is an RTS game being made by a solo developer inspired by the spiritual successor, Empire Earth. The game is focused to have balanced and intriguing gameplay while still keeping it simple for newer players and to help revive and rekindle RTS community surrounding Empire Earth and other 'retro' RTS games.
